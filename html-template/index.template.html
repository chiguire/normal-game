Normal - by Ciro Durán

Everything in this city-in-a-valley is absolutely normal. So why bother enforcing it?

You must keep it at a minimum. Sometimes people will appear in the city, some of the looking for trouble, some of them don't. You will not be able to distinguish between them until they start making damages. Who knows what will they do to keep you on your toes?

One more thing: Tranquilizing non-aggresive groups costs more than aggresive groups.

This game was made for the Global Game Jam 2010, between the dates of January 29th and 31st, 2010. The theme of the jam was "Deception", rollover the following text to find out how waas this implemented (Warning: massive spoiler): As someone obssesed in keeping a normal situation at all times, you find out that you want to eliminate every source of disorder. This city is filled up with sounds, but you will find out that there is a particular sound when someone supposedly disordered appears. You will also find quickly that not always when this sound is played appears a person. You will also find that not everyone wants to fire up the city, and those will eventually go away. Eliminating these cost you more normalness. Also the city will be more restless if you let people turn up fires everywhere. End of statement.

Controls: Your mouse, click anywhere inside the city. You will need you eyes and your ears to scout the city.

Normal uses Flixel for rendering, and sfxr+Audacity for sound effects.
